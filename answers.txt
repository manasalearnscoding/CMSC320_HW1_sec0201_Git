Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Because it is an easy and efficient way to manage version control and collaboration for a project.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science could be applied to finance to explore how different factors interplay with each other to affect market movements.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines two branch histories with a new merge commit, but preserves the original history. Rebasing rewrites a branch's history by adding commit from one branch onto the target branch.
